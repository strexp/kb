# VOIP Service

## Telephone

Telephone numbers are redacted and available upon request.

## Fax

- `424008034001` (public)
- `424008034001` (private)

Our network hosts 2 software fax modems capable of receiving faxes in parallel via queues.

Faxes sent to public number are saved in server and can be viewed at: [fax.gensokyo.dn42](https://fax.gensokyo.dn42)

## DN42 PSTN Bridge

Call `+1 424 242 9915` and follow the guide to dial numbers in DN42.

Caller IDs are translate to: `"Gensokyo Tele42 Proxy for {callerid}" <424008038100{callerid}>`
